Sae Niijima is a 24 year-old Japanese female public prosecutor working for the Tokyo Special Investigation Department. She is the older sister of Makoto Niijima, a student at Shujin Academy and a member of the Phantom Thieves of Hearts.
Sae serves as a central antagonist for a significant portion of the game's story, relentlessly hunting the Phantom Thieves before her own heart is changed, leading her to become a crucial ally. In Persona 5 Royal, she is the Confidant of the Judgement Arcana.

- Her father was a police officer who died in the line of duty, which heavily influenced her career path and her cynical worldview.

- She is the only Palace ruler in the game who manages to have a change of heart and redeem herself without the Phantom Thieves directly stealing her Treasure.
- Her name, "Sae" (冴), can mean "clear" or "skillful," reflecting her sharp intellect.
- She has a professional, and often strained, working relationship with the detective Goro Akechi.
- Her implied Treasure is her deceased father's police journal, an object that represents her original, pure sense of justice before it became corrupted.
